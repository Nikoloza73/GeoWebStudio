Who We Are

Two devs from Georgia,
building for the world

We're Giorgi and Nika — two friends from Tbilisi who met at university and discovered we shared the same obsession: building things on the web that actually look and perform great.

We got tired of seeing local businesses stuck with outdated, slow, or generic websites. So we started GeoWebStudio — a small studio with big standards. We keep our client list intentionally small so every project gets our full attention.
